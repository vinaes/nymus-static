# Nymus — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 10, 2026

## Summary

Nymus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data Collection

Nymus does **not** collect:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Chat messages or conversation history
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Crash reports

## On-Device Processing

All AI inference runs locally on your device using downloaded language models. Your conversations never leave your device and are not sent to any server.

## Network Usage

Nymus connects to the internet only to:

- Download the list of available AI models (from GitHub)
- Download AI model files you explicitly choose to install

No user data is transmitted during these requests.

## Data Storage

All data (conversations, settings, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device. Nymus uses no cloud storage, no user accounts, and no remote databases.

Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

## Third-Party Services

Nymus does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Nymus does not collect data from any users, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted here with an updated date. Since Nymus collects no data, changes are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: **support@vinaes.co**
